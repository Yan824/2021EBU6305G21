National South-West Associated University
1937.11.1-1946.7.31
School Introduction

National Southwest Associated University (NSAU) is a comprehensive university which was relocated to Kunming after the Chinese War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression. On November 1, 1937, national Changsha Temporary University was established by national Peking University, National Qinghua University and private Nankai University in Changsha. It opened in Changsha (this day also became the anniversary of southwest Associated University). In mid-February 1938, with the approval of the Ministry of Education of the Republic of China, Changsha Temporary University moved west in three ways to Kunming. In April 1938, it was renamed National Southwest Associated University.
