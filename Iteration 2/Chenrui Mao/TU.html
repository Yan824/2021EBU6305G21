Changsha Temporary University

On July 7, after the Lugouqiao Incident, the Nanjing Nationalist government held a series of meetings in Lushan mountain to discuss the war situation. The presidents of Peking University, Tsinghua University and Nankai University did not return to Beijing and Tianjin immediately after the lushan meeting, but stayed in Nanjing and Shanghai temporarily. On July 29 and 30, Nankai University was bombed by Japanese planes and most of its buildings were burned down. On August 28, the Ministry of Education of the National Government sent letters to Zhang Boling, president of Nankai University, Mei Yiqi, president of Tsinghua University, and Jiang Menglin, president of Peking University, appointing three people as members of the preparatory Committee of changsha Provisional University.
On September 10, decree No. 16696 of the Ministry of Education officially announced the establishment of Changsha National Temporary University. In October, more than 1,600 teachers and students from the three schools arrived in Changsha after a long journey. October 25, changsha National Temporary University officially opened. The school headquarters is located in the leek Garden in the east of Changsha city. It mainly rents bible College and Hande Girls' School.
On November 1st, classes began at Changsha National Temporary University. This day was later designated as the anniversary of the founding of the National Southwest Associated University. The temporary university integrated the original institutions of Tsinghua University, Peking University and Nankai University, and set up 17 departments. As of November 20th, there are 1452 students in total, including 631 students from Tsinghua University, 342 students from Peking University and 147 students from Nankai University. 114 students are newly recruited and 218 students are on loan. There are 148 faculty members, including 73 from Tsinghua university.
