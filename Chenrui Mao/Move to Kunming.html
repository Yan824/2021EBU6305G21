Move to Kunming

On January 20th, the 43rd Standing Committee made a resolution that the winter vacation will start on the same day and classes will be held in Kunming next semester, and stipulated that teachers and students should register in Kunming before March 15th.

In the middle of February, Changsha Linda began to migrate to Kunming. On February 19, bible college of teachers and students in changsha leek garden (on loan by in large buildings), pledging conference began moving process, due to the inland transportation difficulties in wartime, female classmates and a weak male students by YueHan railway in yunnan to guangzhou via Hong Kong, Vietnam, part of the students along the xiang-gui highway by liuzhou to guilin, nanning, Vietnam in yunnan; More than 200 male students organized a group tour of Hunan, Guizhou and Yunnan, covering 3,200 miles and lasting 68 days, across hunan, Guizhou and Yunnan provinces.

On March 18, due to the shortage of school buildings in Kunming, Zheng Tianting, then the secretary general of Peking University, arrived in Mengzi as a preparatory member of Pku for the establishment of a branch school of National Southwest Associated University.
On April 2, The Ministry of Education ordered The National Southwest Associated University to be renamed as The National Southwest Associated University (NLUU), and set up 5 colleges of arts, science, engineering, Law, Business and normal education, 26 departments, two specialized courses and one elective course. The campus is now located at Yunnan Normal University.
On April 19, the Standing Committee decided to establish a Self-Learning Office of National Southwest Associated University. By the end of April, the preparation of mengzi Branch school was completed.
On May 4th, national Southwest Associated University (SWU) officially opened.

On June 8, the "National Southwest Associated University" was inaugurated on July 1. The school is no longer called "temporary", indicating the long-term nature of the war of resistance.
In August, mengzi branch school moved to Kunming. On August 4, the Standing Committee decided that, in accordance with the order of the Ministry of Education, the UnGA would set up a new normal college starting next semester.
On December 21, the 98th Standing Committee decided to appoint the presidents of the three universities as the chairman of the Standing Committee for a one-year term. Mei Yiqi, president of Tsinghua University, was appointed as the chairman of the standing Committee this academic year. After Jiang Menglin, Zhang Boling are in Chongqing, only Mei Yiqi stayed in Kunming for a long time, so there is no implementation of the rotation system, has been by Mei Yiqi as chairman, leading school affairs.
