Strategic stalemate

After the fall of Guangzhou and Wuhan in late October 1938, the War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression entered a stage of strategic stalemate. Japan in view of the front is too long, the strength is insufficient, were forced to adjust the policy of aggression against China, gradually will be the main forces to fight in the battlefield behind the eighth route and new fourth armies, while to the kuomintang government to give priority to with political lure into surrender, battlefield behind gradually become the main battleground of the Anti-Japanese War, he took the responsibility of leading the Chinese people beat Japan alone.
