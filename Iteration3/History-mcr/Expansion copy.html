Expansion of the school

In July, the three schools began recruiting graduate students. On September 19, the Standing Committee of National Southwest Associated University requested the Ministry of Education to allocate special funds for the establishment of secondary school, primary school and kindergarten attached to normal College.

On November 13, after discussion at the 161st Standing Committee, it was decided to establish the Xuyong Branch School of National Southwest Associated University, with Yang Zhensheng as its director. Xuyong Branch school moved back to Kunming in October 1941.
