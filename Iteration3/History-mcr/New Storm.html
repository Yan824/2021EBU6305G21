New era is coming

The war of liberation broke out. In the War of Liberation, the Chinese People's Liberation Army under the leadership of the Communist Party of China completely destroyed the reactionary regime of the Kuomintang, basically completing the most important historical task of China's democratic revolution against imperialism and feudalism. The People's Liberation War led by the Communist Party of China is a rare majestic drama in the history of Chinese war and world war. A new era of China is coming.
