One Day When We Were Young
One Day When We Were Young features 16 "post-90s" experts and scholars, including Yang Zhenning, Xu Yuanchong, Pan Jiluan, Yang Coix, Wang Xiji and Ma Zhitu, with an average age of over 96 years old, telling the audience their stories of teaching and studying to save the country and serve the nation.
Forever Young
The film tells the story of four young people (one of them is a student in NSAU) of different generations, full of expectations for their youth, but also in the midst of contradictions and struggles due to the changes of the times, who eventually find their true selves.
Documentary of NSAU
Made to commemorate the 80th anniversary of the founding of NSAU in Yunnan, this five-part film uses diaries, articles and interviews with people to recreate a series of NSAU's journey from the preparation and relocation of the school to the daily teaching, life and research of its staff and students.
NSAU Anthem
On July 3, 1938, the Ministry of Education ordered all schools to submit their school songs and school mottos, and in October, the Standing Committee of NSAU established the "School Song and School Motto Committee", appointing Feng Youlan, Zhu Ziqing, Luo Changpei, Luo Yong and Wen Yiduo as members, with Feng Youlan as the chairman.
