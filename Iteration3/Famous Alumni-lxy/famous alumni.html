Introduction

Southwest Associated University is known as "a miracle in the history of higher education in China", and it is also a story in the history of world education.
It only lasted 8 years, but it has an impact to this day. Among the more than 8,000 students of Southwest Associated University, 2 Nobel Prize winners, 8 winners of two bombs and one star merit award, 5 winners of the highest national science and technology award, 9 party and state leaders, 172 winners Academicians and more than 100 humanities masters.
